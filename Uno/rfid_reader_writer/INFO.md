## Connections
### Arduino to board

3.3v GPIO --> Left power rail - Postive \
First ground GPIO --> Left power rail - Negative

5v GPIO --> Right power rail - Postive \
Second ground GPIO --> Right power rail - Negative

SCL GPIO --> 30A \
SDA GPIO --> 29A

13 GPIO --> 2A \
12 GPIO --> 4A\
~11 GPIO --> 3A \
~10 GPIO --> 1A

~3 GPIO --> 14I \
~6 GPIO --> 17I \
2 GPIO --> 20I \
~5 GPIO --> 7A
